How to Reach Us

We do not have a contact form. Sovereign-grade engagements are not initiated through web interfaces — they are initiated through people who know people.

I
Reach out directly

Write to us at the address below with a brief description of your mandate — its nature, the principal involved, and the timing. We respond to every credible inquiry.

II
Mandate definition call

If there is potential fit, we schedule a confidential conversation — typically 30 to 60 minutes — to understand the mandate, the decision-maker's context, and the constraints on timing and scope.

III
Engagement structure

Where fit is confirmed, we propose an engagement structure: scope, deliverables, timelines, and the confidentiality protocols that govern the work. No engagement commences without agreement on all four.

IV
Commencement

Work begins when the mandate is defined, the structure is agreed, and the distribution rules are in place. Not before.

Mandate Fit

The mandates
Istawra is built for

Sovereign decision weight — The mandate involves decisions that carry national or institutional consequence — not divisional or project-level decisions.

Principal-level sponsorship — The engagement is commissioned by or reports to a head of state, minister, board chair, or equivalent sovereign principal.

Multi-stakeholder complexity — The mission spans entities, jurisdictions, or sectors that cannot be governed through conventional management hierarchy.

Genuine ambiguity — The right answer is not known and cannot be Googled. The value we add is in the architecture of how to think about and resolve the question.

Confidentiality requirement — The mandate requires a level of discretion that rules out large consulting teams, open brief processes, or broad internal distribution of work product.

If the mandate fits these criteria, we want to hear from you. If it does not, we will tell you — and if appropriate, suggest who might be better placed to help.
